Fright & Day

Web / Branding / Identity / Personal Project

To elevate the experience of watching scary movies, Fright & Day emerges as the premier web-based, horror streaming service that takes advantage of virtual reality, physical accessories, and a vibrant brand identity to engage horror fans on every possible dimension. View the prototype!

The search tab provides a segmented experience where users can see their viewing history, suggested content, and promotion all at a glance. Users may also filter their results by monster, year, director, and subgenre.
