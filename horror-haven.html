Horror Haven

In response to the low volume of horror-related media on popular streaming sites such as Netflix or Hulu, Horror Haven emerges as a media streaming service that caters to fans of the horror genre. Optimized for all devices, the web app allows to users search & stream movies, TV shows, or internet videos. Search results can be filtered by relevant horror-fanatic criteria such as type of monster, director, and year. Creating an account allows one to assign favorites and create playlists.
