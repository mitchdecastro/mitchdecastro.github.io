In response to the low volume of horror-related media on popular streaming sites, Horror Haven emerges as a media streaming service that caters to fans of the horror genre. Upon becoming a member, one can search through hordes of scary films, TV shows, and internet videos. Updates are featured at the Hub, which showcases seasonal content, trailers, news, and extras. Search results can be filtered by relevant horror-fan criteria such as type of monster, director, and year. Custom lists can also be created for bookmarking and future viewing. Try out the prototype!
