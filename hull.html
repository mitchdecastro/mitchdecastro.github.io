HULL

HULL is a lifestyle and clothing brand that produces wares designed for the true challenges of commuter environment such as cramped trains, freezing temperatures, or the infamous Monday Mornings. By visiting the online store, users can create accounts to add items to their carts or check on incoming orders. Users may also check the status of their orders without creating an account as well as check the weather for the upcoming days. Products are organized by function as opposed to design style, allowing users to search for what items are appropriate for their needs.
