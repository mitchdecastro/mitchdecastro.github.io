HULL

UI Design / Identity / Branding

HULL is a lifestyle and clothing brand that produces high-quality wares designed for the true challenges of the outdoor environment. Targeting customers who are frequently on-the-go, whether commuters or travelers, HULL's online presence immediately brings visitors to the store. Products are organized by function as opposed to design style, allowing users to search for what items are appropriate for their needs. Try it out!
