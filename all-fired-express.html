All-Fired Express

With the goal of supplying the public with delicious meals, the All-Fired Express food truck combines the best of fire-grilled dishes with the efficiency of high-tech drones. By downloading the app, users can view the menu, find the nearest All-Fired food truck, and make orders when in vicinity to have them be delivered via drone.
